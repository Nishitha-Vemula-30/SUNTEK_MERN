Bangalore

Bangalore, officially known as Bengaluru, is the capital city of Karnataka and one of the fastest-growing cities in India. It is widely known as the “Silicon Valley of India” because it is a major hub for information technology and startups.

Many multinational companies like Infosys, Wipro, and IBM have offices here. The city is famous for its pleasant climate throughout the year and beautiful parks like Lalbagh and Cubbon Park. Bangalore is also known for its vibrant nightlife, diverse culture, and delicious South Indian cuisine. It perfectly combines modern development with natural greenery.
