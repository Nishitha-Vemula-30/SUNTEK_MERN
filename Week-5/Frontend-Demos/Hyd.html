Hyderabad

Hyderabad is the capital city of Telangana and one of the fastest-growing metropolitan cities in India. It is known for its rich history, beautiful monuments, and delicious food.

Today, Hyderabad is a major IT and business hub of India, home to areas like HITEC City and Gachibowli, where many global companies are locatedThe city is famous for the Charminar, Golconda Fort, and Salar Jung Museum. Hyderabad is also called "The City of Pearls" because of its historic pearl and diamond trading centers. In recent years, it has become a major IT hub, earning the name "Cyberabad" due to companies like Microsoft, Google, and Amazon.p>
